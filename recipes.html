Tropical Paradise

Ingredients:

1 cup pineapple juice

1/2 cup orange juice

1/4 cup lime juice

1 tablespoon honey or agave (optional, adjust to taste)

Your Quatro flavour of choice

Ice cubes

Pineapple slices and mint leaves for garnish



Instructions:

In a pitcher, combine the pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, and honey or agave. Stir well to combine. Fill a glass with ice cubes. Pour the pineapple juice mixture into the glass, filling it about 2/3 full. Top off the glass with Quatro, allowing it to mix with the other ingredients. Stir gently to combine the flavors. Garnish with a slice of pineapple and a few mint leaves for an extra touch of freshness. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing mocktail Feel free to adjust the sweetness and tartness of the mocktail by adding more or less honey/agave and lime juice, according to your taste preferences.
